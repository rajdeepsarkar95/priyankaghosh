About the project

The Moto g100 project focused on designing the user interface and experience for Motorola’s “Ready For” feature — a tool that allows users to wirelessly connect their smartphones to larger screens, transforming the mobile experience into an immersive desktop or gaming environment.

Main screens

Design Process

The design process followed a user-centered approach, aiming to enhance usability, clarity, and visual consistency across the interface. A cohesive color guide was established, aligning with Motorola’s brand identity and providing a solid visual foundation.

Final result

The Ready For project turned the Moto g100 into a powerful productivity tool. With a user-centered approach, we delivered a fluid, scalable, and intuitive experience across work, entertainment, and video calls. The solution boosted product value and positioned Ready For as a key differentiator for Motorola.
